LAYOUT INSTRUCTIONS:
- Header: Name, contact info (email, phone, LinkedIn, location) at the top
- Sections in this order: Summary, Experience, Education, Skills
- Experience: reverse chronological, company name + title + dates on same line
- Each role: 3-5 bullet points with strong action verbs
- Skills: comma-separated list grouped by category
- Education: degree, institution, graduation year
- Clean and simple — no decorative elements
